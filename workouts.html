Training Programs

Warm-up Routine

Beginner
  • • 5 min light jogging
  • • Dynamic stretching (leg swings, arm circles)
  • • High knees (30 seconds)
  • • Butt kicks (30 seconds)
  • • Hip circles and shoulder rolls

Sprint Drills

Intermediate
  • • 10 x 40m sprints (30 sec rest)
  • • Ladder drills for agility
  • • Cone weaving sprints
  • • Hill sprints (5 x 20m)
  • • Cool down walk (5 minutes)

Core Workout

Intermediate
  • • Plank hold (3 x 60 seconds)
  • • Russian twists (3 x 20 reps)
  • • Mountain climbers (3 x 30 seconds)
  • • Leg raises (3 x 15 reps)
  • • Bicycle crunches (3 x 20 reps)

Leg Strength

Advanced
  • • Squats (4 x 12 reps)
  • • Lunges (3 x 15 each leg)
  • • Deadlifts (4 x 8 reps)
  • • Calf raises (3 x 20 reps)
  • • Box jumps (3 x 10 reps)
